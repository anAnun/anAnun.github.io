PRIVACY POLICY

Lyrics Notepad privacy policy.

By using this app, you consent to this privacy policy.

  • We require permission to use the microphone for recording audio
  • We require permission to use the phone storage to save audio recordings, and export content to backup your created content.
  • We do not collect or share data.
  • App does not have advertisements
  • App is upgradable to pro version for $1.99 which unlocks the apps full potential
  • All of the notes that is created are stored in the app's internal storage, and can only be accessed from within the app. However, with proversion, you are able to export all of your notes into a .json file and save to to whereever you decide.
  • All of the recordings will be stored in the devices cache if you do not have the proversion. Once you have the proversion you will be able to export all of your recordings into your internal storage, and all recordings from then on will be saved in your internal storage, as long as you have the proversion.
  • App uses datamuse api for rhymes.
